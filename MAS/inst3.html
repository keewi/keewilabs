Instructions: For this activity, your group will work together to memorize and match different stimuli. At the beginning of each round, a "+" will appear to signal that the activity is about to begin. When it disappears, a sequence of 10 symbols will appear on the screen, one at a time. On the last screen, you will be asked to identify the 10 symbols shown from a pool of symbols. The %Accuracy for all of your group members will be averaged to calculate how many points your group gets. There will be 1 practice round and 10 scored rounds.

Press Next to continue.
